# List of instances blocked by corteximplant.com

This list contains all instances that have been muted or blocked by corteximplant.com.

If you think that an instance is unfairly on this list, please contact [@lucy](https://corteximplant.com/@lucy) (Mod) from **CORTEX IMPLANT**. An " appeal " cannot be made against instances which are blocked with the reasons § 86a and § 184b - German Criminal Code.

The purpose of this list is to be transparent towards our users, and to collaborate with other admins on identifying those instances that violate the Code of Conduct, or those that tend to contain bad actors, and who boost unwanted content into other instances. Instances that don't exist anymore are removed periodically.

## Data source  
 
Our blocklist is based on reports from our users and from the following sources:

- [List of instances blocked by chaos.social](https://github.com/chaossocial/about/blob/master/blocked_instances.md)
- [Faraday's Fediverse Blocklist](https://github.com/F4R4DAY/faradays-fediverse-blocklist)
- Public Fediverse messages about bad actor instances (Instances will be checked beforehand)
- Publicly visible blocked fediverse instances (Instances will be checked beforehand)

If you are interested in a proof, please contact [@lucy](https://corteximplant.com/@lucy). 
Some requests may NOT be answered due to legal reasons.
